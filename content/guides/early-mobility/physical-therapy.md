## Professional Organizations for SLP

Joining a professional association can add to your skills by providing access to continuing education, association literature and resources, and allow for professional networking and sharing via events and conferences.

* [American Academy of Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation](http://www.aapmr.org/)
* [American Board of Physical Therapy Residency and Fellowship Education](http://www.abptrfe.org/home.aspx)
* [American Board of Physical Therapy Specialties](http://www.abpts.org/home.aspx)
* [American Council of Academic Physical Therapy](http://www.acapt.org/)
* [American Physical Therapy Association](https://www.apta.org/)
* [Commission on Accreditation in Physical Therapy Education](http://www.capteonline.org/home.aspx)
* [Federation of State Boards of Physical Therapy](https://www.fsbpt.org/)
* [Foundation for Physical Therapy](http://www.foundation4pt.org/)
* [MD Board of Physical Therapy - License Verification](https://mdbnc.dhmh.md.gov/bptverification/default.aspx)
* [Physical Therapy Political Action Committee](http://www.ptpac.org/)
* [World Confederation for Physical Therapy](http://www.wcpt.org/)
